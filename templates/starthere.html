First and foremost, I’d like to say thank you for taking the time to visit my site. My name is Kevin and I’m well aware of the numerous excellent websites across the internet that have no doubt helped many board game enthusiasts dip their first brushes in paint. Despite this I’ve felt the need to create a beginners guide but with some of the methods I’ve discovered.

Compared to many other painters, I’m still a baby at this. I purchased my first “serious” board game (Gloomhaven) back in early 2019 and started painting my first game (Blood Rage) at the end of 2019. I had no prior experience and even less artistic skills. However, after some time experimenting I was able to paint what you see before you.

My methods are always easy, (relatively) painless and cheap. None of these guides will require what I consider a higher level of dexterity to accomplish. If you ever feel you’ve got a good handle on these skills I encourage you to go visit other sites or even take courses to really up your game! All I aim to do is to help get your feet off the ground. So just relax and enjoy the journey and always be proud of what you create!
